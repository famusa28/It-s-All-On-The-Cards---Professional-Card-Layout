Examples

Walking

Walking is my favorite way to stay fit and clear my head. I love the peace and quiet of nature and how walking helps me relax and exercise at the same time. I always feel refreshed and energized after a good walk.

Volleyball

Playing volleyball is my favorite hobby because it keeps me active and lets me spend quality time with friends. I love the teamwork and excitement of the game, and it helps me stay fit and energized. Volleyball was the first sport I learned to play, and it holds a special place in my heart because of the fun memories and friendships I've built through it.

Badminton

Badminton is one of my favorite hobbies because it's a great way to improve attention and speed. It's my go-to game for fun and fitness, and I always keep a racket handy for free time with my siblings. It's easy to learn, making it perfect for everyone to join in and enjoy.

Sunset Watching

Watching the sunset is one of my favorite hobbies because it’s a calming and beautiful experience. It gives me peace to watch the colors change in the sky, and I have a lot of sunset pictures capturing those moments. I get to watch it at work every day because of my schedule, and I time my lunch to enjoy a drink while watching the sunset.

Reading Books

Reading books is one of my favorite hobbies because it’s a wonderful way to relax and learn new things. I often read before I sleep, and I especially enjoy fiction. Right now, I'm reading Before the Coffee Gets Cold by Toshikazu Kawaguchi. The book is about a small café in Tokyo where customers can travel back in time, but only for the duration of their coffee.
